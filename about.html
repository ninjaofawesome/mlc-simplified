How It Works

1

Fill out the rolodex

A client match means someone who fills out the online form to possibly match with any of the clients we currently have. After you fill out the form, Julie will personally reach out to you if we thing you match with our current clients. We interview eight people per day for a nominal consultation fee. See Terms and Conditions for more details

2

We Capture Your Essence

After the interview, our team creates a customized bio of you. We ask all of our new clients to provide 3-5 photos (including selfies and full body shots) in their rolodex application, supplemented with sample text of our thoughts on you from our interview feedback. We are excellent in honoring you as a person.

3

Stay In Our Rolodex

You are always considered a match for our clients after your interview, unless you let us know we've done our job perfectly. We will constantly and consistently go through files and alert you via email if we think that you may match with anyone we currently have on deck.
